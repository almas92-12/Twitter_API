WeRateDogs

WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that rates dog pictures using pretty unorthodox scale in which more often than not actual rating exceeds standard denominator of 10, with justification of such tomfoolery being "they're good dogs Brent"  along with funny comments.

However, this comedy account is not only a good source for your daily dose of humorous tweets, but with 4 million followers and 5000+ tweets, it is a solid database for curios minds in search for answers to dog related questions. For example, if you are prospective dog owner, the questions, such as “what is the most likable breed of dogs?”, “what is the most popular one?” , and “what is the most popular dog name?” may arise.

However, before jumping into answering these questions, let’s try to find out more about WeRateDogs twitter account itself. Luckily, we have some data, which may help us understand some user habits - what are the hours of increased account activity, what is the preferable platform and how many words per tweet it usually takes to provide chuckleworthy comment.

We can see that there are two spikes in account activity throughout the day - around 1am and 4pm, with most tweets being posted between 3pm and 4am (most likely there is a time zone difference issue).

Whomever runs this account sure loves Iphone since this is the platform that has been used almost exclusively to post tweets.

They say that brevity is the soul of wit, and judging from the fact that it usually takes around 20 words per tweet, I am inclined to agree with this statement.
